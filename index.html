App Gallery QuickApp Engine


As the technical lead in the QuickApp Android project, I took on the responsibility of guiding both development efforts and critical aspects such as code quality and documentation standards. Over a span of three years, I led a team of up to 14 members, managing both technical decisions and resource allocation.

One of the most significant challenges was ensuring backward compatibility throughout the development process. To address this, I introduced improvements in code reviews, requirement analysis, and story design processes, which resulted in a substantial reduction in issue counts. In 2022, these contributions were recognized with the "Quality Star" award, presented by our leadership. Although this award was given to me individually, I firmly believe that the success was a collective achievement made possible by the collaboration and dedication of our entire team.

Quick apps are a new type of installation-free apps developed based on industry standards. Users can use them directly without downloading and installing them. Quickapp Engine enables quickapps to be rendered and used as native android applications.

ITO Online İşlemler

The ITO application was a mobile app offering online services for Istanbul Chamber of Commerce, such as fee inquiries/payments, e-signed activity certificates, registration application status inquiries, and verification.
In this project, developed using Kotlin, I applied clean code principles by implementing the MVVM design pattern. Given the common operations across multiple modules, I created a dynamic structure.

ITO Online İşlemler allows you to handle issues such as membership inquiry/payment, activity certificate (e-signed), registration application result inquiry, and registration application result verification online.
